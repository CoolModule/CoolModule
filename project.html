Query 1:

How many characters not-white are employed in illegal professions?

How many characters not-white are employed in illegal professions?

SELECT ?character
WHERE {
    ?character cont:hasProfession ?profession .
    ?profession rdf:type cont:IllegalProfession .
MINUS {
    ?character cont:hasEthnicOrigin dataset:White .
}
} GROUP BY ?character

Which female characters have both an occupation and a child?

SELECT ?character
WHERE {
    ?character cont:hasGender "female"^^rdfs:Literal .
    ?character cont:hasProfession ?profession .
    ?character cont:hasChild ?child .

Query 3:

Which are the characters that have professions somewhat related to drug?

Which are the characters that have professions somewhat related to drug?

SELECT ?character
WHERE {
    ?character cont:hasProfession ?profession .
    ?profession rdfs:label ?profName .
    FILTER regex(?profName, "drug", "i") .
}

Who are the fictional characters that are not protagonist?

Who are the fictional characters that are not protagonist?

SELECT ?FictionalCharacter
WHERE {
    ?FictionalCharacter ref:type cont:character .
MINUS {
    ?FictionalCharacter rdf:type cont:Protagonist .

Who are the antiheroes that have work relationship the villain?

Who are the antiheroes that have work relationship the villain?

SELECT ?antihero
WHERE {
    ?antihero rdf:type cont:AntiHero .
    ?villain rdf:type cont:Villain .
{?antihero cont:hasBusinessPartner ?villain } UNION {?antihero cont:hasEmployer ?villain }
}
