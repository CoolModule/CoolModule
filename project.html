Query 1:

How many characters not-white are employed in illegal professions?

How many characters not-white are employed in illegal professions?

PREFIX dataset: <http://www.semanticweb.org/ontologies/2021/2/BB_dataset# >
PREFIX cont: <http://www.w3id.org/arco/ontology/content# >
PREFIX rdf: <http://www.w3.org/1999/02/22-rdf-syntax-ns# >

SELECT ?character
WHERE {
    ?character cont:hasProfession ?profession .
    ?profession rdf:type cont:IllegalProfession .
MINUS {
    ?character cont:hasEthnicOrigin dataset:White .
}
} GROUP BY ?character

Result:

dataset:Gus_Fring
dataset:Hector_Salamanca

Query 3:

Which characters have professions somewhat related to drug?

Which characters have professions somewhat related to drug?

Query 5:

Who are the antiheroes that have work relationship the villain?

Who are the antiheroes that have work relationship the villain?

Query 9:

Which are the character with most business partner relationships?

Which are the character with most business partner relationships?

PREFIX dataset: <http://www.semanticweb.org/ontologies/2021/2/BB_dataset# >
PREFIX cont: <http://www.w3id.org/arco/ontology/content# >

SELECT ?characterOne (count (distinct ?characterTwo) as ?count)
WHERE {
    ?characterOne cont:hasBusinessPartner ?characterTwo .
}
GROUP BY ?characterOne
ORDER BY DESC(?count)

Result:

dataset:Tod_Alquist          "5"^^xsd:integer
dataset:Lydia_Rodarte-Quayle          "4"^^xsd:integer
dataset:Jack_Welker          "2"^^xsd:integer
dataset:Mike_Ehrmantraut          2"^^xsd:integer
dataset:Saul_Goodman          "2"^^xsd:integer
dataset:Jesse_Pinkman          "1"^^xsd:integer
dataset:Skyler_White          "1"^^xsd:integer
dataset:Walter_White          "1"^^xsd:integer
